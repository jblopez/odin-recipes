Texas Style Brisket

Description

Once you try this juicy brisket, you’ll be itching for the next opportunity to smoke another one. BBQ (especially brisket) is something you have to learn by experiencing it yourself, so let’s jump in and give it our best go!

Ingredients

  • Brisket
  • Lawrys
  • Ms Dash
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Sausage
  • Aluminum Foil

Steps

  1. Select Your Brisket
  2. Trim The Brisket
  3. Season Your Brisket
  4. Get Smoking
  5. Wrap Your Brisket
  6. Rest Your Brisket
  7. Slice Your Brisket
  8. Eat Your Brisket
